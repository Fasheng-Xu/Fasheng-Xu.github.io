01 / About

Background & contact

Fasheng Xu is an Assistant Professor of Operations and Information Management at the University of Connecticut School of Business. His research develops analytical models of how emerging technologies — generative AI, blockchain, and FinTech — transform operations, supply chains, and financial markets.

His work appears in Management Science and Manufacturing & Service Operations Management, among other journals, and has been recognized with the POMS Early Career Research Accomplishments Award and multiple best-paper honors. He is co-author of The Owner, a business novel about work in the generative-AI era, and serves as an Associate Editor for Decision Sciences.

03 / Generative AI

Working at the AI frontier

Generative AI runs through much of my current research — the economics of foundation models, AI agents and agentic commerce, and how GenAI is reshaping organizations and the future of work. It is also the subject of my first book.

New book · 2026

The Owner

A Business Novel about Work in the GenAI Era

A novel about work, ownership, and what it means to lead as generative AI moves from tool to teammate — following one company through the messy, human reality of an AI transformation. Written with Wei Chen.
